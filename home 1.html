A Taste of Thailand, Made From the Heart

Macquarie Fields' most beloved Thai restaurant, where tradition meets bold, modern flavour.

We take pride in serving traditional Thai cuisine made from scratch, bringing together authentic flavours and warm hospitality in a welcoming, vibrant setting. Whether you're dining with family, catching up with friends, or celebrating a special occasion, Betong Thai promises a memorable meal every time.

Betong Thai has become a local favourite in Macquaire Fields, known for its bold street-food inspired dishes and genuine Thai hospitality. Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat - it's a taste of Thailand's rich culinary tradition, brought to life dish by dish. Every recipe is crafted with care, using fresh ingredients and authentic Thai spices to ensure every bite bursts with flavour.
